Partnership Models

Built around your goals.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. You know your property better than anyone. Choose the model that gives you a win in year one, and let the results make the case for what's next.

Option A
Client Production
Design, fabrication, and installation: full pipeline, one contract. We bring the complete production to your property at premium quality because we own every stage.
Best for: Properties wanting a turnkey production partner and full control of revenue
Option B
Revenue Share
Lower upfront investment. We co-invest in the experience so you don't carry the full load. Revenue is shared, which means our incentives are aligned: we only succeed when you do.
Best for: Properties wanting to minimize capital risk with a partner who's invested in the outcome
Option C
Brand Partnerships
Your corporate partners want to activate on-property. We build the pitch package (decks, renderings, sponsor briefs) so you walk in ready to close.
Best for: Properties with existing brand partners looking to fund experiential activations without drawing on property capital.
